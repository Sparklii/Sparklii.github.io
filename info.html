About Me

My name is Katerina Marie Ibrahim. I'm a model, university student, fashion designer, cosplayer, and front-end web designer currently residing in Miami, USA. I am a signed professional model with Runways the Talent Group in Miami, USA, Element Model Management in Atlanta, USA, with modeling, runway, and film experience. Modeling has always been my dream job, so I couldn't be happier doing anything else. Currently I am studying software engineering at a small engineering university, with a concentration on front-end web development. I frequently design and create cosplay costumes and attend sci-fi/anime conventions, as well as dance costumes and fashion accessories.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book you for a modeling job or test photoshoot/video?
Simply send me a message through my Contact page. For paid work, I will then direct you to one of my agents depending on the city. For unpaid/test work: I am highly selective of who I will model for free for, so please include a link to your portfolio as well as the concept and information about the job. I am also happy to work in exchange for clothing & merchandise.

What modeling experience do you have?
I have experience in modeling for high fashion, editorial, lifestyle, glamour, catalogue, and underwater photoshoots. I have been printed in several magazines (high fashion & glamour), websites (catalogue & lifestyle), and had a speaking role in a national commercial campaign for Robust Energy Drink. I have starred in several model/fashion videos, as well as walked in runway shows for Atlanta and New York designers.
