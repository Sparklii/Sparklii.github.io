My name is Katerina Marie Ibrahim. I am originally from Atlanta, USA. I'm signed with Runways the Talent Group in Miami, and have been published internationally in editorials and fashion films. My main hobby is handmaking cosplay costumes to attend sci-fi/anime conventions, as well as creating fashion accessories and other arts and craftsy things. I studied Software Engineering in university, and designed and coded my personal website.

How can I book you for modelling work?
Simply send me a message through my Contact page. For paid work located within Florida, you will need to contact Runways the Talent Group. For unpaid/test work: I am highly selective of who I will model for free for, so please include a link to your portfolio as well as the concept and information about the job. I'm also happy to work in exchange for clothing & merchandise!

What is your workout routine?
It's pretty easy, actually! A set of abs and squats every day, cardio every other day, and some yoga thrown in once a week and I'm good.
The most important part is what you eat. I try to stay within a specific range of calories per day. Exercise doesn't burn nearly as many calories as you think, so simply eating healthy makes things a lot better.
