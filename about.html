My name is Katerina Marie Ibrahim. I am originally from Atlanta, USA. I'm signed with Marquee Model Management as my mother agent, Purple Model Management in India, and Runways the Talent Group in Miami, and have been published internationally in editorials and fashion films. My main hobby is handmaking cosplay costumes to attend sci-fi/anime conventions, as well as creating fashion accessories and other arts and craftsy things. I studied Software Engineering in university, and designed and coded my personal website.

What is your workout routine?
It's pretty easy, actually: a set of abs and squats every day, cardio 2-3 times per week, and some yoga thrown in once a week. My personal favorite way to get my cardio in is Dance Dance Revolution!
